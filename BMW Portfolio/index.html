M4 Competition

The 2025 BMW M4 Competition Coupe features a TwinPower Turbo inline-6-cylinder engine producing 510 hp and 479 lb-ft of torque, coupled with an 8-speed M Steptronic transmission and M xDrive all-wheel-drive. It accelerates from 0-60 mph in about 3.4 seconds and has a 12.3-inch digital gauge cluster and 14.9-inch touchscreen infotainment system. Other specs include 4-wheel disc brakes, multilink suspension, and seating for four.
